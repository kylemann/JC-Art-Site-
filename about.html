Jorge Caceres

Artist

Jorge Luis Caceres was born in Lima,Peru in 1978. His multi-cultural background gives him the unique ability to convey his creativity through painting. As a self-taught artist, He expresses himself by combining abstract pieces of different colors and shapes.

He studied Architecture at one of Lima's most renowned universities but found himself being drawn to the freedom that painting offered him. He participated in several art competitions and won third place for one particular work of a city landmark. The painting was highly acclaimed and was featured in a local newspaper.

Jorge finds his country to be a big source of inspiration. His extensive travels to different cities such as Arequipa and Cuzco particularly reflect those cultural influences in his artwork. His visits to the ruins of Machu- Picchu exposed him to the wonderfully organic shapes and the views offered by this wonder of the world.

In 2001 he moved to Washington DC where he currently resides. He has been commissioned to paint private collections and has shown his paintings in galleries such as, Touchstone Gallery, among various others.
